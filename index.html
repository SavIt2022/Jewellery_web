About us

Incorporated in Kenya in 2022, Ease International Culture Management Limited (Registration number PVT-6LUKAEQG) is a private limited company which engages in agent sales of cultural products. The headquarter of the Company is based in China, with which the business scope and investment involving Guizhou, Beijing, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Chengdu, Sanya, Changsha, Shandong, Tibet, Hong Kong, Taiwan and other provinces and cities in China,as well as Thailand and other countries and regions.

The company adheres to the enterprise concept of "Civilization, Honesty, Efficiency and Innovation" and is willing to work hand in hand with all sectors of society to cooperate, invest, develop, build, seek common development and create a better tomorrow.

Culture & Arts

On the basis of collecting collections for many years, the company has formed a collection of Precious Jade Hallto treasure art series,which is composed of jade,gold, antique wild pearls (Dongzhu), jadeite, gem, porcelain, Tangka and calligraphy and painting. The artwork investment provide the collectors all around the world with a perfect platform to meet their demands for the collection. The time period of the collections ranges from Hongshan Culture of Neolithic to modern society, gathering celebrity calligraphy and painting, demonstrating the styles of different dynasties. No matter from the historical value, artistic value or academic research value, those art collections should all be treated and recognized as rare artistic treasures.
